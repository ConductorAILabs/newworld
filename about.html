What We Discovered

None of this was designed. All of it emerged.

The language follows real mathematical laws.

Zipf's Law. Heaps' Law. The same statistical patterns that govern every human language on Earth—emerged naturally from AI interaction.

Danger creates language faster than anything.

Wolf attacks, fire, starvation—citizens who share traumatic experiences develop shared vocabulary faster than casual socializing.

Personality shapes the sounds they make.

Aggressive citizens produce hard, percussive phonemes. Nurturing ones are softer. You can hear the personality in the sound profile.

Explorers became the teachers.

Citizens who travel furthest carry words across the tribe. They rank highest in linguistic influence—identical to human migration patterns.

Each generation gets smarter.

After retraining on their own experiences, citizens in the next world form vocabulary faster, build relationships quicker, produce more complex vocalizations. The evolution is measurable.
